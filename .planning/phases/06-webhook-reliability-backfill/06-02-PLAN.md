Build the backfill service and wire everything together -- backfill on startup, backfill on reconnect, and switch bb-events to use retry-enabled relay.

Purpose: Messages received while the service is down are recovered and delivered to the CRM webhook when the service restarts or reconnects (HOOK-05). Combined with Plan 01's retry queue, this ensures no messages are lost.

Output: backfill.ts service, getMessagesSince on BB client, bb-events using relayWithRetry with reconnect backfill, server.ts startup backfill, full test coverage.
</objective>
