If attempts >= maxRetries, remove and logger.error({messageId: entry.payload.messageId, attempts: entry.attempts}, 'Retry exhausted, discarding webhook delivery'). Else recalculate nextRetryAt = Date.now() + calculateDelay(entry.attempts).
       - destroy(): clearTimeout(timer), timer=null, queue=[]
